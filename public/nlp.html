NLP Analysis

Ready to see how your resume stacks up?

We will be using Google's Cloud Natural Langauge API to see how effective your resume is!

Why Natural Langauge Processing?

We can examine the effectiveness of our resume by looking at your choice of words.

One way is through entity analysis. We can classify your words based on grammar and how they are percieved. You can check for use of effective verbs and numbers in your bulletpoints!

Tip: Have verbs at the beginning and include numbers to quantify your impact!

Tip: Specifically mention technologies/skills used and the context!

Another way is through content category analysis. We can place the categorize your resume into subjects based on its contents. You can check what industries/skills you are portraying.
